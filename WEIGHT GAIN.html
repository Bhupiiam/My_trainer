ABOUT GMSA

GMSA Academy teaches Govt. of India Fitness Trainer Level 4, Personal Trainer (Beginner), Nutrition Consultant courses, including RPL (Recognition of prior learning). If you have a prior experience as a gym trainer or gym coach without certificate then you can apply for RPL and get certified which increases the value including salary. This certificate will be issued directly by Indian Govt. GMSA will be starting few more courses in 2019.
